Solemnity Two. A follow up to the popular Solemnity template is built around the same principles as the original template, just with a slightly different look to it. This template has been designed with a slightly more 'bloggy' feel in mind. And so, I introduce you to; Solemnity Two!

Introduction

Hello and welcome to Solemnity Two, the follow up to my Solemnity template. As mentioned in the description, this template is built on the same fundamentals as its predesecor - calm, easy on the eyes and highly readable. Of course it also makes use of valid xhtml and css too.

The template was created by Six Shooter Media and the icons used are that of the open source icon sets available at famfamfam.com.
